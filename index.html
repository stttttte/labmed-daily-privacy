隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 2 月 22 日

概述

LabMed Daily（以下简称"本应用"）是一款检验医学论文聚合阅读工具。我们高度重视您的隐私，本政策说明了我们如何处理您的信息。

我们不收集个人数据

本应用不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人身份信息，包括但不限于：

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话号码
  • 设备标识符或广告 ID
  • 位置信息
  • 使用行为数据或分析数据

数据来源

本应用通过以下公开 API 和 RSS 源获取论文元数据（标题、作者、摘要、发表日期）：

  • PubMed E-utilities API（美国国立医学图书馆）
  • ScienceDirect RSS Feeds（Elsevier）
  • Wiley Online Library RSS Feeds
  • MDPI RSS Feeds
  • Taylor & Francis RSS Feeds
  • OpenAlex API

所有数据均来自公开可用的论文元数据，本应用不提供论文全文，点击论文将跳转至出版商官方网站。

本地存储

您的收藏、已读记录和设置偏好仅保存在您的设备本地（使用 SwiftData），不会上传至任何服务器。删除应用将同时删除所有本地数据。

翻译功能

本应用使用 Apple 系统内置翻译框架（Translation Framework），翻译过程完全在设备本地完成，不会将内容发送至第三方服务器。

无广告与无追踪

本应用不包含任何广告、第三方追踪 SDK 或分析工具。

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集任何儿童的信息。

隐私政策变更

如本政策发生变更，我们将在本页面更新内容并修改生效日期。建议您定期查看本页面。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Overview

LabMed Daily ("the App") is a laboratory medicine literature aggregation tool. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

We Do Not Collect Personal Data

This App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Data Sources

The App retrieves article metadata (titles, authors, abstracts, publication dates) from publicly available APIs and RSS feeds, including PubMed E-utilities API, ScienceDirect, Wiley, MDPI, Taylor & Francis, and OpenAlex. No full-text articles are stored; tapping an article opens the publisher's official website.

Local Storage

Your bookmarks, read history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. No data is uploaded to any server. Deleting the App removes all local data.

Translation

The App uses Apple's built-in Translation Framework. All translations are processed entirely on-device and no content is sent to third-party servers.

No Ads or Tracking

This App contains no advertisements, third-party tracking SDKs, or analytics tools.

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
